📖 How to Use This Dashboard

Quick reference for directors, staff, and anyone new to the system.

👑 If you're Heidi or Nikkieta
  • Start on Executive View every time
  • Update the Snapshot tile monthly
  • Add / remove Focus Items as priorities shift
  • Use YOY Analytics to spot trends
  • Use All-Hands View at all-hands meetings
  • Add big items to the Leadership Calendar
📋 If you're a Department Director
  • Go to Department Data Entry
  • Select the correct month at the top
  • Fill in your department's numbers
  • Click Save [Your Dept]
  • Do this once a month (or more if helpful)
  • You can go back and fill in past months too
🎤 If you're at an All-Hands Meeting
  • Go to the All-Hands View tab
  • Select the month to present
  • This shows only org-wide, non-sensitive stats
  • Hit Print / PDF to share as a handout

🗂 What Each Tab Does

▾
🧭 Executive View
The home base. High-level snapshot tiles, department pulse, leadership focus, and upcoming calendar items. This is the page Heidi and Nikkieta open every time.
📋 Department Data Entry
Where directors enter their monthly numbers. Always select the correct month first. Past months can be filled in at any time.
📈 YOY Analytics
Pick any department and metric and see a chart comparing this year vs. last year (or any years you choose). The more data you enter, the more useful this gets.
📅 Leadership Calendar
Big-picture events only — reviews, major expenses, events, policy rollouts. If it wouldn't go on a physical desk calendar, it doesn't belong here.
💸 Money Rhythm
Not accounting — just a visual map of big income and expenses by month. Great for reducing surprise anxiety.
🎤 All-Hands View
A clean version of the dashboard for all-hands meetings. No sensitive data — just org-wide highlights, pipeline flow, and YOY callouts.
🔒 Leadership Notes
Password-protected. For Heidi and Nikkieta only — pipeline timing observations, sensitive notes, and future metrics not ready to share with departments yet.
🅿️ Parking Lot
Brain dump for ideas, needs, and items not yet active. When adding an item, include your name and which department(s) need the help so leadership knows where to route it. Prevents things from getting lost without cluttering the main views.
⚙️ Manage Fields
Button in the Data Entry tab. Lets anyone add, rename, or remove metrics from any department.

❓ Common Questions

▾
Can I enter data for a past month?
Yes — use the month selector in the Data Entry tab to pick any past month, fill it in, and save. It will appear in the YOY Analytics history.
Can I add a new metric to my department?
Yes — click the ⚙️ Manage Fields button in the Data Entry tab.
My number looks wrong on the Executive View.
The Executive View snapshot tiles pull from the most recent saved entry. Go to Data Entry, confirm the correct month is selected, and re-save.
Does this save automatically?
Click the Save button on your department card after entering numbers. Data saves within your browser session — for permanent storage across devices, ask Heidi or Nikkieta about next steps.
What is the Incident Log for?
The Incident Log captures org-wide safety events in one place — bites, medication errors, staff injuries, animal escapes. It keeps incidents visible at the leadership level without burying them in department data.
